Episode 1: The Diamond Necklace:
Guy de Maupassant

Download MP3 Download OGG

Summary: Mathilde Loisel, a middle-class French woman, dreams of royalty and riches. She finally gets invited to a ball, and borrows some jewelry from a friend to wear at the event. But after the ball, the neckalace goes missing, and and Mathilde and her husband must decide what to do.

Episode 3: The Queer Feet:
G. K. Chesterton

Download MP3 Download OGG

Summary: A gentlemen's club meets in a fancy restaurant for their ceremonial dinner. The most important part of the dinner is the fish course, which they eat with their precious set of silver forks. However, after the dinner, they discover the silverware has been stolen. The mystery is solved by a Catholic priest named Father Brown.

Download MP3 Download OGG

Summary: Two "desprate criminals" Sam and Bill, kidnap a kid named Johnny, who loves pretending to be an Indian named "Red Chief." Sam and Bill try to hold Johnny for ransom, but they find that Johnny is more trouble than they thought.
