Episode 3: The Queer Feet:
G. K. Chesterton

Download MP3 Download OGG

Summary: A gentlemen's club meets in a fancy restaurant for their ceremonial dinner. The most important part of the dinner is the fish course, which they eat with their most precious posession: their set of gilded silver forks. However, after the dinner, they discover the silverware has been stolen. The mystery is solved by a Catholic priest named Father Brown.
