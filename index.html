Welcome to the Classic Bookshelf Podcast

The Classic Bookshelf Podcast features classic, public domain short stories, read by Nathanael Bayne. Each episode features a new story by authors like Charles Dickens or G. K. Chesterton. The stories can be serious, funny, or scary, but they are all tales that deserve to be listened to. I hope you enjoy listening to this incredible podcast.

Do You Have an Idea for a Story?

If you have an idea for a short story I should read for a future episode, click the link and then fill out the form. If your idea is accepted, I will contact you to let you know. In the episode with that story, I will mention your name in the introduction.

Join the Show: Read in an Episode

Do you want to read in one of The Classic Bookshelf Podcast episodes? To Join the Show, look at the future episodes, and choose a character you want to do. Then, follow the instructions to record your voice, fill out your information, and upload the audio file. Then, send in the form. I will listen to the audio file and contact you to if you have been accepted to be on the show.
